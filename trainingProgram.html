FOUR ALPHA ORGANIZATIONAL CREATIVITY TRAINING PROGRAMS There is a great void when it comes to using creativity to solve problems in corporate America. Most companies have no formal structure for generating ideas or innovative thinking. But today, the rapid changes and agility demanded by technology and information glut require vast amounts of creativity to answer never-before-encountered questions. The Four Alpha Process of Organizational Creativity was established to fill that void. Creativity At Work outlines the step-by-step process required to implement formal creative thinking structures throughout your organization. Beyond this book, Four Alpha offers training programs to organizations customized to fit their needs, beginning with a Needs Assessment evaluation.

Four Alpha Needs Assessment – Introducing Organizational Creativity to Your Organization The Four Alpha Needs Assessment evaluation consists of the following:
  • Assessment of your company's current performance and development of a learning plan that applies organizational creativity training in several components of your organization. In this learning plan, we help you to define what you want to learn about making your company more creative, why you want to learn it and how long you need to develop the skills required to successfully apply organizational creativity to your business.
  • Access to online training resources on Four Alpha concepts:
    • – Pilot courses on Four Alpha organizational creativity topics to help you evaluate our program.
    • – Lecture notes, case studies and exercises to gain the essential skills and knowledge needed to determine which organizational creativity topics are best suited for your company.
  • Process mapping of your organization's workflow where specific organizational creativity inputs and outputs can be applied.
